About a Juan Grijalba

I am a Software Engineer focused on the complete software lifecycle, from backend architecture to deployment automation. My primary expertise lies in the Java ecosystem with Spring Boot, where I design scalable and resilient solutions aligned with strategic business objectives. I have recently integrated DevOps into my workflow, specializing in containerization with Docker, orchestration with Kubernetes, and CI/CD pipeline automation. My goal is to bridge the gap between development and operations to deliver high-quality software more frequently and reliably. I have a C1 level of English, enabling me to collaborate effectively in international teams and demanding technical environments.

Tech Stack & Skills:

  • Backend: Java (Spring Boot), Node.js, Python, C#
  • DevOps & Cloud: Docker, Kubernetes (K8s), GitHub Actions, CI/CD Pipelines
  • Data: PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB
  • Tools & Culture: Git, Agile (Scrum/Kanban), Jira, Microservices Architectures
  • Security: Implementation of JWT standards, OAuth2, and OWASP best practices.
